25th Anniversary Celebration Concert – The 1812!

The 25th Anniversary celebration concert was held at Queen's Gardens on Sunday the 2nd of August. It was presented by Townsville city council and the Australian Festival of Chamber Music(AFCM) and hosted by Damien Beaumont from ABC Classic FM. See the gallery for images of the night!
